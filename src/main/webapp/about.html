About Us

Technovation Technologies Limited, was established to provide information technology solutions for medium and large sized businesses. Technovation is a technology intensive organization that is geared towards the delivery of high-tech solutions in areas of integrated business solutions, system applications, automation, customer experience, business resilience, standardization of core backend systems, and communication & network management services. We have a broad range of practical IT skills and knowledge and a track record for delivering cost-effective IT solutions and services. We also strive in leading our clients on their digital transformation journey, providing innovative next-generation technology solutions and services that leverage deep industry expertise. Technovation provides clients with a comprehensive range of solutions including Infrastructure consultancy, onsite/offsite services, custom software development, web and portal development, software and web application testing, application integration and enterprise architecture consulting.
